## Communication Rules

---

### 1. Be Polite and Professional
> Always greet Members politely.  
> *Use appropriate language and maintain a professional tone in communication.*

---

### 2. Be Responsive
> Provide an initial response or acknowledgment of message/ticket as quickly as possible.  
> *Communicate estimated handling time when possible.*

---

### 3. Gather Required Information
> Request relevant details such as device, executor, in-game map, supporting evidence, etc.  
> *Ensure sufficient information before proceeding with handling.*

---

### 4. Provide Clear and Concise Instructions
> Give step-by-step guidance for easy understanding.  
> *Avoid excessive technical jargon unless the Member understands it.*

---

### 5. Follow Up
> Check back with the Member after providing a solution.  
> *Offer additional help if the issue is not resolved.*

---

### 6. Escalate When Necessary
> If the issue is beyond your authority or knowledge, escalate to higher Staff.  
> *Inform the Member that the issue is being escalated with an estimated response time.*

---

### 7. Maintain Confidentiality
> Do not share Member information, tickets, or internal discussions with unauthorized parties.  
> *Ensure all data is handled securely.*

---

### 8. Stay Updated
> Follow developments in rules, scripts, updates, and common issues.  
> *If unsure, ask senior Staff rather than providing incorrect information.*

---

### 9. Be Patient and Empathetic
> Understand that Members may be frustrated or confused.  
> *Show patience, empathy, and remain helpful in every interaction.*
