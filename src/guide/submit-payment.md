### <:app:1381680319207575552> Cara Submit

1. Masuk ke **thread ticket** customer
2. Ketik `/submit-payment`
3. Isi form nya:
> **amount** → nominal (contoh: 19999, 50000)
> **currency** → IDR atau USD
> **customer** → tag discord customer
> **method** → metode bayar (BCA, Dana, dll)
> **details** → tulis nama script yang dibeli
> **proof1** → screenshot bukti tf
> **proof2** → (optional) bukti tambahan

---

### <:OLOCK:1381580385892171816> Abis Submit

- Payment masuk ke channel approval
- Customer bakal dapet DM dari bot
- Di thread juga bakal ada notif

---

### <:money:1381580383090380951> Work Report

Setiap payment yang di-approve otomatis kesimpen ke database!

> **Submit payment** = Rp 2.500 / ticket
> **Approve payment** = Rp 1.500 / whitelist

Cek statistik kerja kamu pake `/work-stats`

- Pastiin bukti tf keliatan jelas
