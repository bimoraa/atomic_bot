## Helper and Supporter Guide

Welcome! This guide covers everything you need to know about Helper tickets.

---

### Helper Tickets - What Are They?
Available for all members who need help with:
- Script features and usage
- General product questions
- Technical support (non-purchase)

**Not for:** Payment issues, refunds, or moderation matters

---

### Your Role (Voluntary)

Helpers are **volunteers**. You help when you want to and can.

<:checkmark:1417196825110253780> Join tickets when you have time
<:checkmark:1417196825110253780> Share your knowledge and experience
<:checkmark:1417196825110253780> Choose which tickets to help with
<:checkmark:1417196825110253780> Step back anytime if busy or unsure

No pressure. No requirements. Help at your own pace.

---

### Cannot Handle? Redirect:

- Refunds → Customer Support
- Sharing permissions → Staff
- Executor keys/links → Public resources
- Payment issues → Customer Support
- Member reports → Moderation

Example: "This is outside Helper scope. Please contact Staff/Customer Support."

---

### Tips

<:checkmark:1417196825110253780> Help only when comfortable
<:checkmark:1417196825110253780> Be polite and clear
<:checkmark:1417196825110253780> Don't know everything? That's okay
<:checkmark:1417196825110253780> Ask Staff when unsure
<:checkmark:1417196825110253780> Quality over quantity

---
